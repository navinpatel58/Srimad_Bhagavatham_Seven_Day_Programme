Programme overview

Each day of the programme focuses on a specific theme, unfolding the essence of Bhagavata Dharma—devotion, surrender, and the path to spiritual fulfilment.

You can navigate directly to each day’s detailed page, where you’ll find the text, explanations, and reflections for that session.
